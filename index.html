How it works

Wordoir gives you six hidden words. Each guess is evaluated against all rows at once, so matching letters can help multiple answers.

  • Pick any row and enter a 5-letter guess.
  • Green letters lock in for the row they belong to.
  • Letters shared across rows appear when your guess contains them.
